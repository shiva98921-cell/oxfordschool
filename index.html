— About Our School

Where every child finds their spark.

Oxford International School, Meerut is a co-educational, affiliated institution offering quality education from Pre-Nursery to Class VIII. Located in the peaceful neighbourhood of Shatabdi Nagar, our campus is designed to feel like a second home — bright classrooms, a safe play area, and teachers who know every child by name.

We believe education is more than textbooks. It is the art of awakening curiosity, building character and shaping the leaders of tomorrow — proudly Indian, globally aware.

📚

Strong Academics

Concept-based learning with regular assessments, doubt-clearing sessions and personal mentoring.

🎨

All-Round Growth

Music, dance, drawing, drama, sports and yoga — a child blooms in every direction.

🛡️

Safe Environment

CCTV-monitored campus, trained female staff, and a zero-tolerance policy on bullying.

🌱

Indian Values

Sanskar Shiksha — discipline, respect for elders, and pride in our culture.

— A Word From The Principal

Every student is a seed — and every seed deserves the right soil.

Dear Parents,

Welcome to Oxford International School. As an educator, my deepest belief is simple — children are not vessels to be filled, but lamps to be lit. Our role is to nurture that light with patience, love and the right values.

At Oxford, we work hand-in-hand with parents to build children who are academically strong, culturally rooted and emotionally confident. We celebrate small wins, correct gently, and always look at every child as an individual.

I invite you to visit our campus, meet our teachers, and see the joy of our children — because seeing is believing.
